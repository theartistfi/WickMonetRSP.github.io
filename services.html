《Residency Support Programs at Wick Monet》

Residency programs for artists are fantastic opportunities that offer time, space, and sometimes funding for artists to focus on their work, engage with a community, or explore new ideas. Many of these programs are specifically designed to support the artistic process, whether you’re working in visual arts, writing, music, or other creative fields. They often provide a variety of resources, including studio space, accommodations, and access to professional networks.

《SCHEDULING & BOOKING》

The Residency Support Program provides artists with an opportunity to focus on their creative work in a conducive environment, while receiving access to studio space, accommodations, and other essential resources. The program aims to support professional growth, experimentation, and networking with peers in the arts community.
